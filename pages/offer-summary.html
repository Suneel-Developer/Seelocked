Brand New

Please select one of the following:

Factory Sealed

A device in this condition should be brand new and factory sealed in its original packaging. It should not be resealed, activated, or opened. Refurbished devices do not fall under this category. In short:

  • Brand new and never opened
  • Not activated
  • In original, undamaged packaging with matching IMEI/serial numbers

Open Box

A device in this condition must still be brand new and fully functional in its original retail packaging, but does not need to be sealed. Refurbished devices and devices in packaging designed by the carrier (or any non-OEM packaging) do not fall under this condition. In short:

  • Brand new but opened and/or activated
  • Must still have original plastic/sticker covering the device itself (if this plastic has been removed or reapplied, please do not select this condition)
  • No scratches (including the smallest micro-scratches) or any signs of use
  • 100% battery health and 0 battery charge cycles
  • In original, undamaged packaging with matching IMEI/serial numbers and unused accessories

Has the device ever been used?

Mint/Good Condition

A device in this condition should be fully functional with light to no signs of use. Additionally, the following should be true:

  • No excessive or deep scratches
  • No scuffs or dents on the frame
  • No cracked or bent frames
  • No cracks, chips, or LCD damage (i.e. burns, spots, lines, etc.)
  • All parts are 100% original
  • Battery health is above 80%

Note: For devices with an after-market or non-original screen, please select "LCD Damage" as its condition

Poor Condition

A device in this condition is fully functional with more visible signs of use. This can include some deep scratches and more excessive wear. For devices with extreme scratching/wear, please choose "Cracked" as its condition. Additionally, the following should be true:

  • No cracks, chips, or LCD damage (i.e. burns, spots, lines, etc.)
  • No bends or cracks on the frame
  • All parts are 100% original
  • Battery health is above 80%

Note: For devices with an after-market or non-original screen, please select "LCD Damage" as its condition.

Cracked

A device in this condition must be fully functional but with screen damage. This includes:

  • Screen cracks
  • Small chips
  • Dead pixels and LCD burns
  • Excessive scratches

Note: For devices with larger chips or screen separation, please choose "LCD Damage" as its condition.

Which part of the back is cracked or severely scratched?

LCD Damage

A device under this category can have any kind of damage to its LCD (i.e. burns, spots, lines, etc.) and/or front glass, but must still be otherwise fully functional. Additionally:

  • Touch damage is OK, but if we are unable to test your device for basic functionality such as "Facial Recognition" or making a phone call, we may reach out to you with a revised quote.
  • After-market screens are OK (whether damaged or not).
  • No bends or cracks on the frame.
  • Battery health must be above 80 percent.

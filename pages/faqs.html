Frequently Asked Questions

General

  • What happened to "iBuyLocked"?

    We're still the same company! We just changed our name to "SellLocked". Our staff and workflow remain the same.

  • Do you buy locked devices?

    Yes! That's what we specialize in. If you're having trouble identifying your device's lock type, please reach out to a representative and we'll do the best we can to help.

  • Do you buy devices that aren't locked?

    Yes! If your device doesn't have any lock types associated with it, sell it under our “no locks” category.

  • Is your company real? How do I know I won't get scammed?

    Yes - we're real and ready to buy your device! But don't just take our word for it, see our hundreds of 5-star reviews that date back all the way to March of 2019. If we were anything but trustworthy, someone would've spoken up by now.

  • Do you buy devices that are not listed on your website? Like iPads, androids, and AirPods?

    Yes! We're working hard to bring other devices to the front page of our website. In the meantime, you can reach out to our customer service department if you have a device for sale that is not listed on our website. We can purchase iPads, high-end androids, airpods, apple watches, etc.. There are some devices that we aren't able to purchase, but it never hurts to ask!

  • Can you buy my box or accessories if I include them in my shipment?

    Unfortunately, we aren't able to offer any additional value for your used device's box or accessories, but we'd still be happy to accept them! :)

Process

  • How does the process work?

    Our process is designed to be as simple as possible for our users.

    1) Start by selecting your device or devices from our website and completing all steps that follow, to get a quote.
    2) Once you accept our offer, you'll receive an automated email from us with a prepaid UPS shipping label attached to it, as well as instructions on how to properly prepare your device before sending it to us.
    3) Lastly, mail your device to us and wait for your transaction to be concluded.

    It's that easy! We'll email you once your transaction has been concluded and payment has been made, or if there are any issues with your transaction. If there is an issue with your transaction we'll offer you a revised quote which you can either accept or reject. If you reject our offer, we'll mail your device back to you for free.

  • How long does the process take?

    Time in transit varies based on your location. However, once your package is delivered to us, payment will be made within 1-5 business days (usually under 48 hours). If you're curious about UPS's estimated time in transit, you can take a look at their "U.S. Ground Maps"; we're in Arizona.

  • Will I receive a confirmation email once my package is received?

    We'll email you once your transaction has been concluded and payment has been made, or if any issues arise during inspection. We won't, however, email you once we've received your package. To check if we've received your package, please track it on UPS's website. Once your package has been marked "delivered" by UPS, we'll have received it. If you'd like to be notified once your device has been received, we recommend opting-in to UPS's text or email notifications. We plan to integrate delivery notifications in the near future.

  • How can I check my transaction's status?

    Please track your package on UPS's website. Once your package has been marked "delivered" by UPS, you can expect to receive an update from us within 1-5 business days (usually under 48 hours). If you haven't received any emails from us, there are no updates yet. Remember to check your "junk/spam" and "all mail" folder in case any of our emails land there. If you have not received any updates for more than 5 business days after your package has been delivered, please reach out to customer support.

Offer/Payment

  • When do I get paid?

    Payment is issued within 1-5 business days (usually under 48 hours) after your package has been marked “delivered” by UPS. Our business days are Mon-Fri.

  • How long is my offer valid for? Can it expire?

    In order for your offer to remain locked in, you must ship your device within 3 business days of creating your transaction. As long as your tracking information shows that it was shipped within 3 business days of creating your transactional, your original quote will be locked in. In other words, your package's time in transit will not affect your price lock.

  • What if you aren't able to honor my original quote?

    If we're unable to honor your original quote for any reason, we'll reach out to you with the option to accept a revised quote, or to have your device mailed back to you for free.

  • If I include my device's original box and/or accessories, will that increase my overall payout?

    Unfortunately, we aren't able to offer any additional value for used devices' boxes or accessories, but we're happy to still take them! :)

  • What payment methods do you offer?

    We offer “Paypal”, “Venmo” and “Check”.

  • Do you send physical checks or eChecks?

    If you select “Check” as your preferred method of payment, we'll physically mail you a paper check that will arrive to you in 1-10 business days via USPS stamp mail.

  • How long does each payment method take?

    If you choose “Paypal” or “Venmo”, your payment will be sent instantly after your transaction has been concluded. As mentioned above, transactions are concluded within 1-5 business days (usually under 48 hours) of your package being marked “delivered” by UPS. If you choose “Check” as your preferred method of payment, you'll receive it within 1-10 business days after your transaction has been concluded.

Shipping

  • Do I need to include my device's box or accessories?

    Unless you're selling us a brand new device, your device's box and accessories are not required. That being said, we always appreciate any accessories or boxes you may want to include, but we won't be able to offer any additional value for them.

  • Do I need to remove my sim-card before sending my device to you?

    You don't need to do this, but we recommend that you do. For “used” devices, we'll remove and dispose of any sim-cards that may be left inside. If your device is brand new, it's up to you to decide whether or not you want to remove it. If you open your sealed device to remove its sim-card, it will no longer fall under our "New (Factory Sealed)" condition. We won't open brand new devices before reselling them, so if your sim-card is still inside of one, the next user will have it.

  • How long does shipping take?

    If you choose to use our prepaid UPS label to ship your device, your package should arrive to us in 1-5 business days (depending on your location). The labels we send you are UPS ground, and it is possible that your package could get delayed in transit, so this is just an estimate. If you'd like to see a more accurate estimate based on your location, take a look at UPS's delivery map for ground shipments here. We're in Arizona (85268).

  • Is my package insured?

    Your package is not insured. If you'd like to insure it, we recommend purchasing your own shipping label. If you choose to purchase your own shipping label, please reach out to customer support with your new tracking number and your transaction number.

  • Can I use my own shipping label? Or do I have to use yours?

    Yes, you can purchase your own shipping label. Whether you're looking to purchase additional insurance or upgrade your package's transit speed, you're more than welcome to purchase your own label. If you do decide to purchase your own label, please reach out to customer support with your new tracking number and your transaction number.

  • What if I can't print my own label and/or find a box to ship my device in?

    We'd recommend visiting your nearest UPS store and having them print and/or package it for you. Fees will apply.
